Kohli’s Early Life and Career:

Kohli was born in Delhi, India in 1988. He started playing cricket at a young age, and he quickly made a name for himself as a talented batsman.

Early Cricket Journey: Kohli’s love for cricket started at a young age when he joined the West Delhi Cricket Academy at the age of nine. He quickly rose through the ranks, impressing coaches and teammates with his natural talent and dedication.

First-Class Debut and Rapid Ascendancy: Kohli’s first-class debut came at the age of 19 for Delhi in 2006.He impressed immediately, scoring a half-century on debut and showcasing his impressive strokeplay. His consistent performances earned him a spot in the India Under-19 team for the 2008 World Cup, where he emerged as one of the tournament’s top performers.
